Summary:

This project analyzes news articles from NPR and PBS and podcast transcripts sources via PodScribe, originally transcribed from public podcasts feeds using Google's Speech-to-Text API.

We respect all data owners' rights and applicable terms of use. We importantly do not redistribute copyrighted source audio.
